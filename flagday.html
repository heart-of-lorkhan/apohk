APO - “Only One Ocean” Flag Day 2022

A Plastic Ocean Foundation (APO) is a charitable organisation registered in Hong Kong, dedicated to creating a wave of change to stop plastic pollution and to be part of the solution through science, education and policy advocacy.

APO will be holding our first flag day in New Territories on 18 June 2022 to raise funds for our ocean conservation program. Help us celebrate the ocean together by supporting this fundraising program!

Event Details

District : New Territories Region
